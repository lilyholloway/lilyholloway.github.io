Lily Holloway (born in 1998, she/they) is a queer postgraduate English student and graduate teaching assistant. Her creative writing has been published in Cordite Poetry Review, Starling, Scum, Landfall, The Pantograph Punch, Midway Journal, and other various nooks and crannies.

Lily is a founding editor for eel mag, an executive editor of Interesting Journal and sits on the boards of the New Zealand Poetry Society and the Same Same But Different queer literary festival.
Lily has a chapbook in AUP New Poets 8 and probably wants to be your penpal. .
